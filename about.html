INSPIRATION

Growing up, my grandmother and mother introduced me to the healing benefits of ginger lemon (lomi) tea.  The name Lomi comes from the Amharic word for lemon (ሎሚ), which is known for its cleansing and healing properties across cultures.  This longstanding staple of healing has been a part of my life since childhood, and it inspired me to incorporate it into my health coaching practice.  In my search for names, I wanted one that would signify the transformative power of health coaching.  The name Lomi reflects this journey.  The colors of lemons, yellow and green, are natural symbols of optimism and newness.  They represent the idea of fresh starts and new beginnings.

ABOUT ELON HAILU

Hi, I'm Elon Hailu, a mindbodygreen Health Coach, and first-generation immigrant from Ethiopia.  I stumbled into the world of health coaching by accident, but it turned out to be my true calling.  After leaving medical school and a long-term relationship, I knew I wanted to pursue a career that not only promoted my own healing but also the collective healing of others.  Health coaching was the perfect fit for me, and I've never looked back.

My passion for healing has been with me from a young age, but it wasn't until I approached it from a more holistic lens that I truly found my calling.  Through my work as a health coach, I help people embrace a more well-rounded and fulfilling approach to their health journey.  I firmly believe that a person's healing journey doesn't begin after a diagnosis or health crisis; rather, it begins in the moments after, when we decide to take action to improve our lives.

As a health coach, my job is to empower my clients to make those changes and turn their health goals into reality.  During our sessions, we'll tap into your strengths and successes to inspire more positive changes.  I take a person-centered approach, focusing on your unique needs, goals, and challenges.  Whether you're struggling with stress management, healthy eating habits, or simply want to feel more energized and balanced, I'm here to guide and support you every step of the way.

As a first-generation immigrant, I understand the unique challenges and pressures that come with navigating a new culture and trying to find your place in the world.  That's why I'm passionate about helping others embrace their own individual paths and achieve their personal goals.  Let's work together to create a healthier, happier, and more fulfilling life for you.
