Health Coaching

Growing up, my grandmother and mother introduced me to the healing benefits of ginger lemon (lomi) tea.  The name Lomi comes from the Amharic word for lemon (ሎሚ), which is known for its cleansing and healing properties across cultures.  This longstanding staple of healing has been a part of my life since childhood, and it inspired me to incorporate it into my health coaching practice.  In my search for names, I wanted one that would signify the transformative power of health coaching.  The name Lomi reflects this journey.  The colors of lemons, yellow and green, are natural symbols of optimism and newness.  They represent the idea of fresh starts and new beginnings.

WHY health coaching?

Health coaching is for anyone who needs help in changing their habits and behaviors in order to achieve their health goals. One of the best things about health coaching is that you get to choose the goals that matter most to you. If you're not sure where to start, do not worry! I can help you identify your goals and create a plan that fits your lifestyle and preferences.

WHAT is health coaching?

Health coaching is a profession that utilizes proven behavioral change theories to help individuals achieve their health and wellness goals. taking a whole person approach to health, recognizing that health is not just the absence of disease, but a state of complete physical, mental, emotional, environmental, financial, and spiritual well-being. My coaching approach is based on proven behavioral change theories, such as Motivational Interviewing and Appreciative Inquiry in order to empower clients in making long-term, sustainable, changes in their health.

WHO should sign up?
